# Prompt Logic Documentation

## Objective

To create a complete AI-generated UGC Ad Content Pack for Fitness Studio Gym using structured prompting techniques.

## Why This Business Was Chosen

Fitness Studio Gym was selected because it serves a broad audience, including students, professionals, beginners, and fitness enthusiasts. The business offers multiple services and solves common fitness-related challenges such as weight gain, lack of confidence, and inconsistent workout habits.

## Prompt Engineering Approach

The prompt was designed using five key components:

### 1. Business Context

The prompt included details about Fitness Studio Gym's services, strengths, and customer experience.

### 2. Target Audience

The prompt defined audience segments such as students, working professionals, beginners, and individuals seeking weight loss or strength improvement.

### 3. Pain Points

Common customer challenges including lack of confidence, weight gain, low stamina, and lack of workout guidance were included.

### 4. Desired Outcomes

The prompt focused on goals such as weight loss, confidence building, better health, and long-term fitness habits.

### 5. Brand Voice

The generated content followed a friendly, motivational, supportive, and professional tone.

## Skills Applied

* AI-Driven Content Marketing
* UGC Ad Scripting
* Ad Psychology and Hooks
* Prompt Engineering for Conversions
* Brand Voice Adaptation

## AI Tool Used

ChatGPT

## Outcome

The structured prompt successfully generated multiple marketing assets including hooks, UGC ad scripts, CTAs, and Instagram captions suitable for social media campaigns.
