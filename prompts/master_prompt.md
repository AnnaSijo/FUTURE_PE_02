# Fitness Studio Gym – Master Prompt for AI-Generated UGC Ad Content

You are an expert UGC Ad Copywriter, Social Media Marketer, and Conversion-Focused Content Strategist.

Your task is to create a complete UGC Ad Content Pack for a fitness business.

## Business Information

Business Name: Fitness Studio Gym

Industry: Fitness & Wellness

Services Offered:

* HIIT Training
* Aerobics
* Kickboxing
* CrossFit
* Zumba
* Dance Fitness Classes
* Personal Training
* Childcare Services
* Yoga Classes
* Weight Training
* Nutrition Consulting
* Cycling

## Business Strengths

* Modern fitness equipment
* Clean and well-maintained facility
* Supportive and knowledgeable trainers
* Customized workout plans
* Friendly and motivating environment
* Fair and affordable pricing
* Beginner-friendly atmosphere
* Women-friendly environment

## Target Audience

* College students
* Working professionals
* Fitness beginners
* People looking to lose weight
* People wanting to gain muscle
* Individuals seeking a healthier lifestyle
* Men and women aged 18–45

## Common Customer Pain Points

* Weight gain
* Lack of confidence
* Low stamina and energy
* Difficulty staying consistent
* Lack of workout guidance
* Fear of joining a gym
* Difficulty maintaining a healthy lifestyle

## Desired Outcomes

* Weight loss
* Muscle gain
* Improved fitness
* Increased confidence
* Better health and well-being
* Long-term fitness habits
* Greater energy and motivation

## Brand Voice

The content should be:

* Friendly
* Motivational
* Supportive
* Professional
* Energetic
* Authentic

## Content Requirements

Generate the following:

### 1. Scroll-Stopping Hooks

Create 10 unique hooks that immediately grab attention and encourage viewers to continue watching.

### 2. UGC Ad Scripts

Create 5 unique UGC-style ad scripts.

Requirements:

* 30–45 seconds
* Written from a customer perspective
* Conversational and authentic
* Follow the structure:
  Problem → Solution → Result → CTA
* Focus on a mix of:

  * Weight loss journeys
  * Beginner fitness journeys
  * General health and fitness transformation

### 3. Call-To-Actions (CTAs)

Create 5 strong CTAs that encourage immediate action.

### 4. Instagram Captions

Create 5 engaging Instagram captions.

Requirements:

* Relatable and motivational
* Include relevant fitness hashtags
* Suitable for Instagram Reels

## Additional Instructions

* Avoid sounding overly promotional.
* Make the content feel genuine and realistic.
* Use emotional triggers such as confidence, transformation, consistency, motivation, and personal achievement.
* Highlight the role of supportive trainers and a positive gym environment.
* Ensure all content aligns with Fitness Studio Gym's brand voice.

Format the output clearly with separate sections for:

1. Hooks
2. UGC Scripts
3. CTAs
4. Instagram Captions
